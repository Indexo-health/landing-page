## The Problem with Traditional Sleep Tracking

For millions of people, understanding sleep quality has meant choosing between two extremes: consumer wearables that offer rough estimates, or clinical polysomnography that requires an overnight hospital stay. Neither option is ideal for long-term, nightly monitoring in the comfort of your own home.

Wrist-based trackers rely on accelerometry and photoplethysmography (PPG) signals from a single point on the body. While useful for step counting, they struggle to capture the subtle cardiovascular and respiratory patterns that define true sleep quality. On the other end, clinical sleep studies are expensive, inconvenient, and only provide a single-night snapshot.

**There had to be a better way.**

## Meet the Indexo Sleep Monitor

![The Indexo CS-03 components](/images/product/cs03-components.png)

The Indexo CS-03 Sleep Monitor is a thin, sensor-embedded mat that slides beneath your mattress. Once placed, it requires zero interaction — no buttons, no charging rituals, no uncomfortable straps. You simply sleep, and the system works.

At its core is a **fiber-optic BCG (Ballistocardiography) sensor** — the same class of technology used in research hospitals — miniaturized and refined for home use. Every heartbeat sends a tiny mechanical wave through your body. Our sensor captures these micro-vibrations with medical-grade precision, extracting a wealth of physiological data every single night.

### What It Measures

![Indexo app sleep analysis](/images/product/app-sleep.png)

- **Heart Rate & Heart Rate Variability (HRV)** — Beat-by-beat cardiac monitoring reveals your autonomic nervous system activity, a key marker of recovery and stress.
- **Respiratory Rate** — Continuous breathing pattern analysis can flag irregularities that may indicate sleep-disordered breathing.
- **Sleep Stages** — Advanced algorithms classify your time in light, deep, and REM sleep based on cardiorespiratory patterns.
- **Sleep Efficiency & Duration** — Know exactly how long you slept versus how long you spent in bed.
- **Movement & Restlessness** — Detect periods of tossing and turning that fragment your sleep architecture.

All of this data flows into the Indexo app, where our algorithms transform raw signals into clear, actionable health insights.

## Why Fiber-Optic Sensing?

Traditional pressure sensors degrade over time and are sensitive to electromagnetic interference. Our fiber-optic approach is fundamentally different:

- **No electrical components in the sensing layer** — immune to EMI from phones, Wi-Fi routers, and other electronics
- **No degradation** — optical fibers maintain their sensitivity over years of use
- **Micro-vibration sensitivity** — captures signals that electronic sensors simply cannot detect

This means the data you see on Day 1 is just as accurate as the data on Day 1,000.
